Biography
  • Shomir Banerjee was born in Columbus, Ohio, on Friday, February 23, 1973.  He went to first grade through seventh grade in Louisville, Kentucky, and then began boarding school at The Scindia School in Gwalior, India.  He completed my high school at The Hill School, in Pottstown, Pennsylvania, 
  • and then went on to college at Baylor University, in Waco, Texas.  While at Baylor, he studied electronic engineering and premedical classes.  
  • Following college, he spent one and a half years circumnavigating the globe, using nearly every form of transportation known to mankind.  
  • He got a job in King of Prussia, Pennsylvania when he got back, broke up with his girlfriend, and then began to follow Christ.  
  • After 10 months he returned to live with his parents for nearly a year, 
  • and then began a Master's program with Dr. Ahn at the University of Cincinnati.
