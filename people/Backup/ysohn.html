Biography
  • Young-Soo Sohn received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin in 2001. He is currently working as a senior research associate at MicroSystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati for BioFluidic Chip (BioFlips) Project funded from DARPA.
  • His fields of interest includes,
    Bio-MEMS Device and Bio-Sensor
    uTAS (Micro Total Analysis System)
    Lab on a chip
