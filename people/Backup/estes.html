Biography

  • Matt received his Bachelor’s degree in Bioengineering from the University of California, Berkeley in 2003, with emphasis on tissue engineering, bioinformatics, bioMEMS, and biorobotics. While there he started a RoboCup team, worked for two years with NASA, and completed projects on MEMS-based DNA detection and hemocompatibility.
