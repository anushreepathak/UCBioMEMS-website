Biography

  • Michael completed his Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering in June, 2003.  He joined the BioMEMS group in 2004 and is currently in the third year of his PhD studies.  In addition to his research, Michael serves as a NSF GK-12 Fellow, which allows him to bring his content area expertise into high school classrooms through the development of lessons and activities.  For more information about his work, please visit his online portfolio at www.eng.uc.edu/~mrust
