Biography
  • Kang Kug Lee received his Bachelor’s and Master’s Degree in Materials and Chemical Engineering from Hanyang University in Korea. He also completed another Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering at The Ohio State University in June, 2005. He joined the Microsystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati in 2006 and is currently pursuing his PhD under the research guidance of Prof. Chong H. Ahn.
