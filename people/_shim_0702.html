Biography
  • Joon Sub Shim received his B.S. degree in both Mechanical engineering and Electrical engineering from KAIST(Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology) in 2004. He is now enrolled in a Ph.D. program in Electrical Engineering. He joined the BioMEMS lab in September 2005 and is doing graduate research under the research guidance of Prof. Chong H. Ahn.
