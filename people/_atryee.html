Biography

  • Atreyee Bhattacharyya received her Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from West Bengal University of Technology, India in 2010 and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from University of Massachusetts, Lowell in 2012. She joined the Microsystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati in 2013 and is currently pursuing her PhD under the research guidance of Prof. Chong H. Ahn.
