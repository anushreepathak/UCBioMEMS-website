Biography
  • Michael completed his Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering in June, 2003.  He joined the BioMEMS group in 2004 and is currently pursuing a PhD under the guidance of Dr. Chong H. Ahn. In addition to his research, Michael has served as a NSF GK-12 Fellow, which allowed him to bring his content area expertise into high school classrooms through the development of lessons and activities.  For more information about his work, please visit his online portfolio at www.eng.uc.edu/~mrust
