Biography
Dr. Chunyan Li completed her Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering under Dr. Ahn in 2007. She is cuurently working as a Research Scientist with North Shore Long Island Jewish Health System, New York

Research Interests
  • Micro physical and biosensors for microcatheter application
  • Packaging of micro devices.
  • Piezoelectric polymer materials (PVDF, PVDF copolymers) and devices (tactile/force sensors, IR sensor)
  • Polymer MEMS
  • BioMEMS
