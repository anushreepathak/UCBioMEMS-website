Biography
  • WooSeok Jung completed his Bachelor's Degree in Biomedical Engineering at Yonsei University in Korea in August, 2006.  He had worked at SIEMENS Ultrasound Group Korea that develops medical ultrasound system from Feb, 2006 to July, 2007 as an analog engineer. He joined the Microsystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati in 2007 and is currently pursuing his Ph.D under the research guidance of Prof. Chong H. Ahn
