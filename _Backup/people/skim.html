Biography
  • Sanghyo Kim received his Ph.D. and M.S. degree in Chemical  Engineering from Pohang University of Science and Technology in Korean in 1996 and 1992, respectively. He is currently working as a senior research associate at MicroSystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati for BioFluidic Chip (BioFlips) Project funded from DARPA.
  • His fields of interest includes,
    Polymer Science and Technology
    Water Reducible Polymer Coating Systems
    Polymer Interfaces and Surfaces
    Polymer Based Semi-Conductors
    Polymer - Ceramic Nano-Composites
    Plastic Based Bio-MEMS Device and Bio-Sensor
    Plastic Based Bio-Optical Sensor
