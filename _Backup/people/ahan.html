Biography
  • Arum Han received his B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Seoul National University, South Korea in 1997. He is currently working toward the M.S. degree at the Center for Microelectronic Sensors and MEMS (CMSM), University of Cincinnati, Ohio. 
Research Area
  • Micro-electro-mechanical Systems(MEMS)
  • Bio-MEMS (Microchip Capillary Electrophoresis)
  • Microfluidic Systems
  • Bonding technology in MEMS
  • Simulation using FEM
