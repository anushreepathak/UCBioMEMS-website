Biography
  • Jin-Woo Choi received his B.S. and M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Seoul National University in Korea in 1994 and 1996, respectively. He received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Cincinnati in 2000. He is currently working as a research assistant professor at MicroSystems and BioMEMS Lab at the University of Cincinnati for BioFluidic Chip (BioFlips) Project funded from DARPA.
  • His research interests include magnetic particle separators, micromixers, EHD & MHD devices, microfluidic systems for biochemical detection, micro total analysis systems (u-TAS), bioelectronics, and BioMEMS components and systems for biological applications.
