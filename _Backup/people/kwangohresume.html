Biography
  • Kwang W. Oh received his B.S. degree in Physics from Chonbuk National University, South Korea in 1995 and M.S. degree in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science from the University of Cincinnati, Ohio in 1997. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. at the Center for Microelectronic Sensors and MEMS (CMSM), University of Cincinnati, Ohio.
  • His research interests include micropumps and microvalves, microfluidics, bio-optical microsensors, optoelectronic multichip modules (OE-MCMs), optical I/O couplers, flip-chip bonding, packaging, assembling and bonding techniques, and etc.
  • He is and was involved in DARPA project and P&G project.
