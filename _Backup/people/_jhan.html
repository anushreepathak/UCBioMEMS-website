Biography
  • Jungyoup Han received his B.S. and M.S. degree in Control and Instrumentation Engineering from KoreaUniversity in Korea in 1994 and 1996, respectively. He worked at KARI (Korea Aerospace Research Institute) from 1996 to 2001 as a research engineer. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at the MicroSystems and BioMEMS Lab under the research guidance of Dr. Chong H. Ahn.
